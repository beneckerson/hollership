Our Board

Meet Our Dedicated Board Members

The World's Greatest Scholarship In the Universe Scholarship is led by a group of passionate community members who are committed to supporting the next generation of leaders, innovators, and changemakers in Dare County.

Tyler Johnson

Board Chair

Tyler is a local business owner and community advocate who has been involved with the scholarship since its founding. He is passionate about creating opportunities for young people in Dare County.

Jennifer Baker

Vice Chair

Jennifer is an educator with over 20 years of experience in the Dare County school system. She brings valuable insights about student needs and educational opportunities to the board.

John Duke

Treasurer

John is a financial advisor who manages the scholarship's funds and ensures that we can support as many deserving students as possible. He is committed to financial transparency and sustainability.

Sarah Martinez

Secretary

Sarah is a community organizer who coordinates the scholarship's outreach efforts and application process. She is dedicated to ensuring that all eligible students have the opportunity to apply.

Michael Thompson

Board Member

Michael is a local entrepreneur who brings business expertise and innovative thinking to the board. He is passionate about supporting students who demonstrate entrepreneurial spirit.

Lisa Chen

Board Member

Lisa is a healthcare professional who advocates for students interested in medical and healthcare fields. She is committed to supporting the next generation of healthcare providers in Dare County.

Interested in Joining Our Board?

We're always looking for passionate community members who want to make a difference in the lives of students in Dare County. If you're interested in joining our board or learning more about our work, please get in touch.
