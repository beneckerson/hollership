About Us

Our Mission

The World's Greatest Scholarship In the Universe Scholarship was founded with a simple mission: to recognize and support passionate, creative, driven, and impressive students in Dare County.

We're not concerned about your GPA, but your YOU â€” and that's not an acronym, we really just want to hear from you. We believe that traditional academic metrics don't always capture the full potential of students, which is why our scholarship focuses on the whole person.

Our board takes its hard-earned money and gives it away to students who demonstrate exceptional character, creativity, and drive. We look for individuals who are making a difference in their communities and show promise for the future.

Our Values

Creativity

We value innovative thinking and creative approaches to challenges. We believe that creativity is essential for solving the complex problems of tomorrow.

Passion

We look for students who are passionate about their interests, whether academic, artistic, athletic, or community-focused. Passion drives excellence.

Community

We believe in the power of community and the importance of giving back. Our scholars are committed to making a positive impact in Dare County.

Drive

We celebrate students who show determination and perseverance in pursuing their goals, overcoming obstacles, and pushing boundaries.

Our History

2018

Foundation

The World's Greatest Scholarship In the Universe Scholarship was founded by a group of community leaders in Dare County who wanted to create a different kind of scholarship program.

2019

First Scholars

We awarded our first scholarships to three exceptional students who demonstrated outstanding character, creativity, and commitment to their community.

2020

Expansion

We expanded our program to include mentorship opportunities and additional support services for our scholars.

Today

Growing Impact

We continue to grow our impact in Dare County, supporting more students each year and building a community of scholars who are making a difference.
